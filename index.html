About ITZY

ITZY is a powerhouse K-pop girl group known for their energy, confidence, and chart-topping hits. Formed by JYP Entertainment, the group quickly rose to fame with hits like "Dalla Dalla" and "Wannabe."

Here is ITZY with their latest comeback "Gold" album, with two title tracks, "Gold" and "Imaginary Friend"

Meet the Members

Yeji

Role: Leader, Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist

Yeji is known for her incredible stage presence and fierce charisma. She is the leader of ITZY and the group’s main dancer.

Lia

Role: Main Vocalist

Lia is the group’s main vocalist with a smooth, emotive voice. She is known for her elegance and graceful presence on stage.

Ryujin

Role: Main Rapper, Lead Dancer, Center

Ryujin is known for her powerful rap skills and impressive dance moves. She adds a cool and strong vibe to ITZY's performances.

Chaeryeong

Role: Lead Dancer, Vocalist

Chaeryeong’s passion for dance shines through in every performance. As one of the lead dancers, she plays a key role in ITZY’s choreography.

Yuna

Role: Sub-Vocalist, Lead Rapper, Visual

Yuna is the youngest member of ITZY, known for her cute yet powerful presence. She is skilled in both rapping and singing.
