About ITZY

ITZY is a powerhouse K-pop girl group known for their energy, confidence, and chart-topping hits. Formed by JYP Entertainment, the group quickly rose to fame with hits like "Dalla Dalla" and "Wannabe." ITZY is also known as the "4th Generation K-Pop Girl Group" as they have the most Billboard 200 entries as a girl group, setting new records never done before, having the best selling albums, sold out concerts and having the best Japanese albums. They are also considered the best performers of the 4th Generation as they are extremely talented at performing live, their live vocals along with their intense and complicated choreography proves that they are indeed the best and also an irreplaceable group
